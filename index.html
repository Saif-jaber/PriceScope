About us

At PriceScope, we believe that finding the best deal online shouldn't require opening countless tabs or comparing prices manually across multiple stores. Born out of a simple need to make smarter shopping decisions, PriceScope is a non-profit project built to help users instantly compare product prices across popular platforms like Amazon, Noon, AliExpress, and Shein—all in one place. Our mission is to provide a fast, transparent, and ad-free experience that empowers everyday consumers to shop more confidently. We do not track, sell, or manipulate results. Instead, we rely on real-time data pulled directly from retailers, ensuring that what you see is current and unbiased. PriceScope is open-source and community-driven, committed to remaining free and accessible to everyone.

How It Works

PriceScope simplifies online shopping in three straightforward steps: First, users enter the name of a product they’re searching for—such as “wireless earbuds” or “iPhone 15 Pro”—into the search bar. Once submitted, our system instantly reaches out to supported online retailers and scrapes the relevant product listings in real time. Second, the collected data is processed and filtered to present key information such as product titles, prices, and store links in a clean and comparable format. This ensures you’re not only seeing the lowest price available but also getting access to trusted sources. Finally, users can browse the results, compare deals, and click directly through to the retailer of their choice. No logins, no hidden costs—just fast, accurate, and convenient comparison shopping at your fingertips.
